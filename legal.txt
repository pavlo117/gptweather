Legal Information for the Weather Helper Plugin
Plugin Description
The Weather Helper is a plugin designed to provide weather information. The plugin does not require any form of authentication and does not process any personal information.

Data Usage
The Weather Helper plugin utilizes weather data provided by OpenWeatherMap. This data is used solely for the purpose of providing weather information in response to user queries.

Liability
The developer of the plugin is not responsible for the accuracy or timeliness of the weather information provided. The weather information is provided "as is" and "as available", and its use is at your own risk.
